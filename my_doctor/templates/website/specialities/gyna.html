Frequently Asked Questions

When should I consult a Gynaecologist?

Gynaecologists are medical experts who specialize in womens reproductive health. They treat several common health issues such as irregular periods, menopause, issues pertaining to pregnancy and infertility to name a few. Consult a Gynaecologist online at carefit if you suffer from any of these issues and with the right line of treatment, proper medication and expert care, these health issues can be addressed.


As Gynaecologists are experts of the female reproductive system, if you are facing any issues pertaining to it, you should consider consulting a Gynaecologist online. Google gynecologist near me to discover Gynaecologists near you whenever you face any health issues related to menstruation, pregnancy, and the vagina in general. If you experience any of the following symptoms, you must consider seeking medical help.


Painful periods: Sometimes menstrual periods can be extremely painful. This condition also known as dysmenorrhea although is common, our experts can help alleviate the period pain.


Irregular menstruation: A lack or an imbalance of hormones in your body can be a cause for irregular menstruation and can also lead to missed periods.


Too little or too much bleeding on your periods: Imbalances in the hormone levels can cause you to either bleed too less or too much. Heavy bleeding also known as menorrhagia can result in longer periods.


Lumps on either of your breasts: Breast lumps are not always indicative of a disease. However, they must not be neglected as some breast lumps may also be cancerous. Get them examined now to be rest assured.


Pregnancy-related symptoms: Fatigue, nausea, vomiting, and mood swings are some common symptoms of pregnancy. Feel free to reach out to our ladies specialist doctor.


Abnormal wetness or itching in the vaginal region: Vaginal itching could be caused due to infections. If you experience abnormal wetness or excessive itching, you should get it checked.


Abdominal Pain: If you're facing any abdominal pain such as cramps or a stomach ache and its too much to bear, it might be a good idea to consult a doctor.

What are the conditions that our Gynaecologist treat?

Your health is our top priority and we strive to help alleviate your pain and find solutions to your health problems. If you are facing any of the issues mentioned in the list below, ensure that you get it checked as soon as possible because there is nothing more precious than your health.


PCOD: A hormonal disorder that is widely prevalent in women, Polycystic Ovary Disease or PCOD as its commonly known is a condition that although cannot be cured, can be treated with the right medical help. Common symptoms for this disease include irregular menstrual cycle, obesity, and excessive hair growth. If you are suffering from this disorder or are afraid that you might be, get in touch with our experts today.


Pregnancy Issues: Health problems during pregnancy can put both the mothers health as well as the foetus' health at risk. Regular prenatal care and pregnancy consultation can help reduce the risks involved in pregnancy and ensure that your foetus is completely healthy. Get your pregnancy issues addressed at the earliest with the best medical care at carefit.


Post Pregnancy Issues: While some problems after childbirth are unavoidable, there are some signs of health problems that you must not ignore. Menstrual Problems: Usually women go through their menstrual periods every month without a hassle, but this is not always the case. However, its common to face menstrual problems such as Premenstrual syndrome, Heavy bleeding, irregularity in menstrual periods or even absence. These menstrual disorders can be treated easily with the right medical help.


Thyroid Issues: Are you showing symptoms of thyroid issues or suspect you that you might be suffering from them? Get a Thyroid screening done now at your home. Our expert doctors will correctly identify thyroid issues if any and address them at the earliest.


Abnormal Vaginal Discharge: If you notice anything about your vaginal discharge that isnt usual such as a foul smell, an unusual whitish colour or if you ever experience any discomfort in that region, it may be due to an infection. Get it examined now!


Breast Abnormalities: While lumps in breast can exist for a variety of reasons and arent always indicative of breast cancer. However, in case of any breast abnormalities, its always a good idea to video visit a Gynaecologist to get them thoroughly examined.
