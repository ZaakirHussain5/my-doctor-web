Frequently Asked Questions

Why and when should I consult a Gastroenterologist?

Gastroenterologist specializes in the health of gastrointestinal tract and liver. They treat abdominal pain, blood in stool, hepatitis, ulcers, gallbladder and colon diseases, pancreatitis and perform various endoscopy and colonoscopy procedures. You may need to consult a stomach specialist or a gastroenterologist when you are suffering from the following.


  • -When you have a stomach upset and experience diarrhoea or face some gastric problem or have sudden blood in your stoo
  • -When you experience sudden weight loss or experience digestive problems due to difficulty in swallowing food.
  • -When you have prolonged constipation for several days
  • -If you think you might have gallstones in your gallbladder which is a part of the GI system.
  • -When you experience sudden pain in your hernia

What are the conditions that our Gastroenterologist can treat?

Specialists in this field, treat several conditions that affect the GI system. Some of them have been listed below for your understanding.


Ulcers: these are open sores in your intestine or stomach and can cause some serious stomach pain and uneasiness.


Hepatitis B: this is an infection that occurs in your liver and can cause cancer, liver failure and scarring of the liver. It can prove to be fatal to the individual if it is not treated as soon as possible however it is something that can be cured when diagnosed.


Jaundice: it is essential to get jaundice treated in its early stages before it gets serious and out of hand.


Haemorrhoids: this is a common issue in which the veins in your rectum swell when they become irritated. It is best to get it treated as quickly as you can.

Fatty liver: they can perform endoscopy or colonoscopy procedures to get to the root of the problem
